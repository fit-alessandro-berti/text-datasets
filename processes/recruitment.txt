> **Task:** Produce *one* conversational **recruitment case** as JSON.
>
> ### A. Clustering criterion
>
> Inspect all messages and set `"cluster"` to exactly **one** of:
>
> | Cluster value                                                             | Trigger keywords / phrases (case-insensitive)       |
> | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
> | **Engineering**                                                           | `engineer`, `developer`, `software`, `devops`       |
> | **Sales**                                                                 | `sales`, `account`, `quota`, `prospect`, `pipeline` |
> | **Marketing**                                                             | `marketing`, `brand`, `seo`, `content`, `campaign`  |
> | **Customer Support**                                                      | `support`, `helpdesk`, `service`, `ticket`          |
> | **Other**                                                                 | none of the above keywords appear                   |
> | Use the **first** matching rule; default to **Other** if nothing matches. |                                                     |
>
> ### B. Activities (use *only* these)
>
> 1. Application Received
> 2. Application Categorised
> 3. Recruiter Assigned
> 4. Initial Screening
> 5. Candidate Response
> 6. Technical Interview Scheduled
> 7. Technical Interview Completed
> 8. HR Interview Scheduled
> 9. HR Interview Completed
> 10. Offer Proposed
> 11. Candidate Feedback Received
> 12. Offer Accepted
> 13. Offer Declined
> 14. Onboarding Started
> 15. Hire Closed
>
> ### C. Context-specific activities
>
> For **every** event add `"contextual_activity"` that starts with the exact `activity` value and appends sentiment in parentheses — `(Positive)`, `(Neutral)`, or `(Negative)`.
> Example: `Technical Interview Completed (Positive)`
>
> ### D. Output format
>
> Return **one** JSON object — no Markdown or commentary — with:
>
> * **cluster** – one of `Engineering`, `Sales`, `Marketing`, `Customer Support`, `Other`
> * **events** – array in strictly increasing timestamp order; each event object must contain
>
>   * **activity** – one of the 15 activities above
>   * **timestamp** – full ISO-8601 date-time with `Z` (e.g. `"2025-07-31T14:20:05Z"`)
>   * **contextual\_activity** – starts with `activity`, then space, then sentiment in parentheses
>   * **message** – string **≥ 50 characters**
>     Produce nothing else.
