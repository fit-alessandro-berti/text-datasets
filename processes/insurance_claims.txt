> **Task:** Create *one* textual **insurance-claim case** in JSON format.
>
> ### A. Clustering criterion
>
> Examine all messages and set the `"cluster"` field to **one** of the following, using the first rule that matches (case-insensitive):
>
> | Cluster value            | Trigger keywords / phrases                                      |
> | ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
> | **Auto Accident**        | `auto`, `car`, `vehicle`, `collision`, `accident`               |
> | **Home Damage**          | `home`, `house`, `roof`, `fire`, `leak`, `flood`, `storm`       |
> | **Health Reimbursement** | `hospital`, `medical`, `doctor`, `surgery`, `treatment`, `bill` |
> | **Other**                | none of the above keywords appear                               |
>
> ### B. Activities
>
> Produce **10 – 15 events** using the following general-purpose activity names:
>
> 1. Claim Filed
> 2. Claim Categorised
> 3. Agent Assigned
> 4. Claim Acknowledged
> 5. Documents Requested
> 6. Documents Submitted
> 7. Investigation Started
> 8. Investigation Completed
> 9. Settlement Proposed
> 10. Customer Feedback Received
> 11. Claim Escalated
> 12. Claim Approved
> 13. Payment Issued
> 14. Claim Closed
> 15. Claim Archived
>
> ### C. Context-specific activities
>
> For **every** event add a `"contextual_activity"` that starts with the **exact** `activity` value and appends the derived **sentiment** in parentheses – one of `(Positive)`, `(Neutral)`, `(Negative)`.
> Example: `Documents Submitted (Neutral)`
>
> ### D. Output format
>
> Return exactly **one** JSON object — **no Markdown, no commentary** — with:
>
> * **cluster**: string – one of `Auto Accident`, `Home Damage`, `Health Reimbursement`, `Other`
> * **events**: array (strictly increasing timestamps). Each event object contains
>
>   * **activity** – one of the 15 activities above
>   * **timestamp** – ISO-8601 date-time with `Z` (e.g. `"2025-07-31T10:12:45Z"`)
>   * **contextual\_activity** – starts with `activity`, then a space, then sentiment in parentheses
>   * **message** – free text, **≥ 50 characters**
>     Produce nothing else.
