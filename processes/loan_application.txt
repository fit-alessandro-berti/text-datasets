> **Task:** Create *one* textual **loan-application case** in JSON.
>
> ### A. Clustering criterion
>
> After reading all messages, set the `"cluster"` field to **one** of:
>
> | Cluster value                                                           | Trigger keywords / phrases (match case-insensitive)  |
> | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
> | **Mortgage Loan**                                                       | `mortgage`, `home`, `property`, `house`              |
> | **Personal Loan**                                                       | `personal`, `vacation`, `debt`, `medical`, `wedding` |
> | **Auto Loan**                                                           | `auto`, `car`, `vehicle`, `truck`                    |
> | **Business Loan**                                                       | `business`, `company`, `capital`, `startup`          |
> | **Other**                                                               | None of the above keywords appear                    |
> | Choose the *first* matching cluster; if nothing matches, use **Other**. |                                                      |
>
> ### B. Activities
>
> Produce **10 – 15 events** using only these general-purpose activity names:
>
> 1. Application Submitted
> 2. Application Categorised
> 3. Loan Officer Assigned
> 4. Initial Review
> 5. Documents Requested
> 6. Documents Submitted
> 7. Credit Check Completed
> 8. Risk Assessment Completed
> 9. Offer Presented
> 10. Applicant Feedback Received
> 11. Application Escalated
> 12. Loan Approved
> 13. Loan Funded
> 14. Post-Approval Follow-up
> 15. Application Closed
>
> ### C. Context-specific activities
>
> For **every** event add `"contextual_activity"` that starts with the exact `activity` value and appends the sentiment in parentheses — one of `(Positive)`, `(Neutral)`, `(Negative)`.
> Example: `Documents Submitted (Neutral)`
>
> ### D. Output format
>
> Return **one** JSON object (no Markdown, no commentary) with:
>
> * **cluster** – one of `Mortgage Loan`, `Personal Loan`, `Auto Loan`, `Business Loan`, `Other`
> * **events** – array in strictly increasing timestamp order; each event object contains
>
>   * **activity** – one of the 15 activities above
>   * **timestamp** – ISO-8601 date-time with `Z` suffix (e.g. `"2025-07-31T12:04:00Z"`)
>   * **contextual\_activity** – starts with `activity` then space then sentiment in parentheses
>   * **message** – free text, **≥ 50 characters**
>     Produce nothing else.
